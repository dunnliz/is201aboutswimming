Why Choose Utah Swim Lessons?

We believe everyone should have the opportunity to learn to swim safely and confidently. Our experienced instructors and proven methods make learning easy and fun!

  • Certified & Experienced Instructors: Our team is Red Cross certified and passionate about teaching water safety.
  • Small Class Sizes: Personalized attention ensures rapid progress for every student.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Group, semi-private, and private lessons available mornings, afternoons, and weekends.
  • Focus on Water Safety: We prioritize life-saving skills before stroke technique.

üèä Our Programs & Levels

We offer programs tailored to every swimmer, from infants to adults, ensuring a comfortable and progressive learning experience.

  • Tadpoles (Ages 6 months - 3 years): Parent-and-child classes focused on water comfort and fundamental safety.
  • Minnows (Beginner Youth): Introduction to floating, kicking, and basic arm movements. Focus on independent movement.
  • Dolphins (Intermediate Youth): Developing freestyle and backstroke, increasing endurance, and mastering deep water safety.
  • Sharks (Advanced Youth): Refining all four competitive strokes (Freestyle, Backstroke, Breaststroke, Butterfly).
  • Adult Lessons (Ages 16+): Beginner, intermediate, and fitness-focused classes for adults.
